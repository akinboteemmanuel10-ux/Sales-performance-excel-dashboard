# A BIG RETAIL SALES PERFORMANCE 
### Project Overview

This project analyzes sales data using microsoft excel to track performance across regions and years. key insight were generated on customer behavior, product performance, and revenue trends using pivot tables, formulas, and charts. The analysis provides actionable recomendation to support data-driven decision-making and growth.

### Objectives
- Track sales performance across regions and time
- Top selling product and region
- Identify sales trends and patterns
- Customer purchasing behavior
- Provide data-driven recommendations for management decision

### **Tools used**
- microsoft Excel
- Pivot Table & formating
- Power query
- Charts & Trend analysis

### **Dataset Description**
This dataset contains historical sales records including:
- Date
- Region
- Customer ID
- Customer name
- Product category
- Payment method
- Quantity sold
- Discount
- Revenue
- Profit

### **Data cleaning process**
- Removed duplicated and blanks
- handled missing values
- created calculated fields for analysis
- Standardized data format

### **Visualizations**
- KPI for revenue, profit, quantity and discount
- Revenue generated from customer segements 
- Top selling products and region
- Monthly sales trend
- Slicer for payment methods, Region and customer segment
  (created using Excel pivot charts)

### **Key Findings**
- Based on the datasets it reveals that october records the highest revenue of over $10k+
- Credit card payments method recorded the highest transaction count, contributing over 25k+
- The sales performance dropped in the months of feburary due to the poor sales oft able in the west


### **Recommendations**
- Credit card payments contribution
  credit cards make payments quick and convenient, encouraging customer to buy more often. This increases the overall transaction volume
  where customer are likely to spend more per transaction compared to cash or transfer, boosting revenue.
  
-  Customer's Impact
  The analysis reveals that corporate customers are the highest revenue-generating segment which help boost the volume of revenue,
  creating more valuable product with an affordable price will help increase revenue from other segment.
